Tofu Veggie Scramble

Try this savory and delicious vegetarian dish!

Recipe

  • Prep time: 20 minutes
  • Cook time: 25 minutes
  • Total time: 55 minutes

Ingredients

Assemble the following:

  • 1 portabello mushroom
  • 1 small yellow squash
  • 1 small zucchini
  • 1 package of firm tofu
  • 1 russet potato
  • 3 carrots, peeled
  • 5 cloves of garlic
  • 1 thumb-sized piece of ginger
  • 4 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup mirin
  • 1 tsp salt
  • Air fryer
  • Large bowl
  • Small, flat tray
  • Scissors
  • Cutting board
  • Paper towels or napkins
  • Knife
  • Grater
  • Silicone basting brush

Prep

  1. Using scissors, cut tofu package open, then drain water from package
  2. Place tofu block on cutting board, then press clean napkins/paper towls into tofu to squeeze out excess water
  3. Repeat on both sides of tofu block
  4. Cut tofu into 1-inch cubes
  5. Create tofu marinade by combing 1 tbsp sesame oil, 1/8 cup soy sauce, 1/8 cup mirin, three cloves of garlic (grated), and 1/2 your piece of ginger (grated) into your small tray
  6. Place tofu in marinade tray, then cover the tofu with marinade using your basting brush

Cook

  1. Turn your pan to low-medium heat to start toasting the bread
  2. Place one piece of cheese on top of the bread
  3. Place the three tomatos on top of the cheese, spread flat
  4. Place the second piece of cheese on top of the tomatoes
  5. Complete the sandwich by placing the final piece of bread on top of the cheese, buttered side up
  6. After about 3 minutes on low-medium heat, use the spatula to gently lift the sandwich and check the bread.
  7. When the bread is golden brown, carefully flip the sandwich to toast its other side
  8. Once both sides of the sandwich are golden brown, use the spatula to move the sandwich onto your plate
  9. Serve and enjoy!
