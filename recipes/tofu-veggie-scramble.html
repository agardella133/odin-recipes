Tofu Veggie Scramble

Try this savory and delicious vegetarian dish!

Recipe

  • Prep time: 20 minutes
  • Cook time: 25 minutes
  • Total time: 55 minutes

Ingredients

Assemble the following:

  • 1 portabello mushroom
  • 1 small yellow squash
  • 1 small zucchini
  • 1 package of firm tofu
  • 1 russet potato
  • 3 carrots, peeled
  • 5 cloves of garlic
  • 1 thumb-sized piece of ginger
  • 4 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup mirin
  • 1 tsp salt

Supplies

To cook this recipe, you'll need the following:

  • Air fryer
  • Large bowl
  • Small, flat tray
  • Scissors
  • Cutting board
  • Paper towels or napkins
  • Knife
  • Grater
  • Silicone basting brush
  • Spatula

Prep

  1. Using scissors, cut tofu package open, then drain water from package
  2. Place tofu block on cutting board, then press clean napkins/paper towls into tofu to squeeze out excess water
  3. Repeat on both sides of tofu block
  4. Cut tofu into 1-inch cubes
  5. Create tofu marinade by combing 1 tbsp sesame oil, 1/8 cup soy sauce, 1/8 cup mirin, two cloves of garlic (grated), and 1/2 your piece of ginger (grated) into your small tray
  6. Place tofu in marinade tray, then cover the tofu with marinade using your basting brush
  7. Start the cooking process for the tofu before continuing with the rest of the prep
  8. While the tofu is cooking, prepare the rest of the vegetables: dice potato, cut zucchini and squash into 1-inch chunks, and slice carrots and mushroom into small pieces
  9. Combine all vegetables in the large bowl
  10. Add the remaining tofu marinade, sesame oil, mirin, and soy sauce to your vegetables. Then grate the remainder of your garlic and ginger into the mixture (to taste)
  11. Using your spatula, mix everything together and let the vegetables marinade for 5 minutes

Cook

  1. After 10 minutes of marinating, spread tofu cubes out in one layer over the bottom of the air fryer and cook at 400℉ for 10 minutes
  2. Open air fryer and rotate tofu cubes, then cook for another 10 minutes
  3. Safely remove the tofu from the air fryer and place it to the side
  4. Place the marinated vegetables in the air fryer, being careful not to pour in any excess marinade, then cook at 400℉ for 15 minutes
  5. Open air fryer and rotate vegetables, then cook for another 15-20 minutes
  6. Serve and enjoy!
