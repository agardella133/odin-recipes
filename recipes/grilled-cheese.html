Grilled Cheese

Indulge in one of your childhood favorites with a classic grilled cheese!

You can never go wrong with a good grilled cheese sandwich.

Recipe

  • Prep time: 5 minutes
  • Cook time: 5 minutes
  • Total time: 10 minutes

Ingredients

Assemble the following:

  • 2 slices of cheddar cheese slices
  • 2 slices of your favorite white bread
  • 2 tsp of butter
  • 3 slices of tomato

Prep

  1. Soften butter
  2. Butter one side of each slice of bread
  3. Place one slice of bread in your pan, buttered side down

Cook

  1. Turn your pan to low-medium heat to start toasting the bread
  2. Place one piece of cheese on top of the bread
  3. Place the three tomatos on top of the cheese, spread flat
  4. Place the second piece of cheese on top of the tomatoes
  5. Complete the sandwich by placing the final piece of bread on top of the cheese, buttered side up
  6. After about 3 minutes on low-medium heat, use the spatula to gently lift the sandwich and check the bread.
  7. When the bread is golden brown, carefully flip the sandwich to toast its other side
  8. Once both sides of the sandwich are golden brown, use the spatula to move the sandwich onto your plate
  9. Serve and enjoy!
