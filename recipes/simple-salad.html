Simple Salad

Refresh your palatte with this simple, yet tasty, salad!

Not every dish needs to take a long time to prepare. Ready in minutes, this salad is a great afternoon snack.

Recipe

  • Prep time: 5 minutes

Ingredients

Assemble the following:

  • A container of pre-washed spring mix
  • Avacado slices
  • Shredded cheese of your choice
  • Chopped vegetables of your choice
  • Salad toppings of your choice
  • Salad dressing of your choice

Prep

  1. Place two handfuls of spring mix in the large mixing bowl
  2. Add avacado slices, vegetables, and other salad toppings of your choice
  3. Add salad dressing of your choice, to taste (1-2 tbsp suggested)
  4. Cover with lid, then shake salad to mix ingredients together
  5. Serve using salad tongs, and enjoy!
